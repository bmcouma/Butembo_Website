About Butembo Precious Metals

A licensed, legally registered gold trading company based in Nairobi, Kenya. Connecting African gold with the world since 2021.

Who We Are

Trusted Gold.
Transparent Business.

Butembo Precious Metals is the trading name of Butembo Metals & Logistics Limited, a Private Limited Company incorporated in Kenya on 11 March 2021 under the Kenya Companies Act 2015.

We specialise in sourcing and supplying high-quality gold — 23 karat nuggets at 97% purity and 24 karat bars at 99.9% purity — to verified international buyers across the globe.

Our approach is built on complete transparency. Buyers are welcome to travel to our office in Kilimani, Nairobi to physically inspect and test the gold before making any financial commitment.
