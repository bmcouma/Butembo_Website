The Process

How to Buy Gold from Butembo

A clear, step-by-step process from first contact to completed transaction. No surprises, no hidden steps.

1

Contact Butembo Precious Metals

Reach out via WhatsApp at +254 798 227 336, email at byronmireri043@gmail.com, or through the contact form. Share what product you need, the quantity, and your destination country.

2

Receive Full Details & Quote

We respond within 2 hours during business hours with current availability, pricing, and an invitation to visit Nairobi for physical inspection. A full cost breakdown including shared logistics is provided.

3

Travel to Nairobi for Inspection

Visit our office in Kilimani, Nairobi and personally inspect and test the gold. Bring your own testing equipment or we arrange independent assay testing. No financial commitment required at this stage.

4

Agree Terms & Payment

Once satisfied, we agree on quantity, price, and shared costs. Payment via Bank Wire T/T, Letter of Credit, Cash, Escrow, MoneyGram, D/P, or D/A. Currencies: USD, EUR, JPY, CAD, AUD, HKD.

5

Logistics & Delivery to Destination

Smelting, freight, insurance, tax, and all logistics costs are shared and agreed beforehand. We coordinate delivery to your destination and support the full process through to safe arrival.
